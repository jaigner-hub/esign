Sign PDFs for free.
No account needed.

esign is a free, open-source desktop app that lets you add typed signatures and text to any PDF — entirely on your device, with no data sent anywhere.

Download View Source on GitHub

Why esign?

🔒

100% Offline

Your documents never leave your computer. All processing happens locally — no server, no cloud, no account.

✍️

Typed Signatures

Choose from 6 handwriting-style fonts to create a natural-looking signature from your name.

📝

Text Fields

Add dated text, initials, or any other text to any page of your document.

🖱️

Drag & Drop

Place signatures and text anywhere on the page by dragging. Resize them to fit perfectly.

⚡

Fast & Lightweight

Built with Tauri — a tiny Rust core with a webview frontend. Much smaller than Electron alternatives.

💚

Free & Open Source

MIT licensed. No trial periods, no paywalls, no usage limits. Fork it, modify it, make it your own.
