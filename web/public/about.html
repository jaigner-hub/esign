Frequently Asked Questions

Is my PDF signature legally binding?

Yes. Under the U.S. ESIGN Act (2000), the EU eIDAS Regulation, and similar legislation in most countries, electronic signatures carry the same legal weight as handwritten signatures for most documents. FreeSign.ink produces standard PDF annotations recognized by Adobe Acrobat, Preview, and all major PDF readers.

Do you store my documents?

No. FreeSign.ink is a browser-based PDF signing tool. Your files are processed entirely on your device using JavaScript. We have no servers that receive, store, or process your documents. Your privacy is absolute.

What types of documents can I sign?

Any PDF file — contracts, agreements, tax forms, school forms, NDAs, lease agreements, invoices, and more. If it's a PDF, you can sign it with FreeSign.ink.
